Frequality Asked Questions

  • How does warranty work?
    One of the reasons why Kurian Renovations is a leading renovation company in Canada is that they offer a 2-year warranty on all their work. This means that if you encounter any issues or defects with the work done by Kurian Renovations, they will fix it for free within 2 years of completion. This shows that Kurian Renovations is confident in their quality and craftsmanship, and that they care about their customers’ satisfaction. With Kurian Renovations, you can rest assured that your renovation project is in good hands.
  • What about Pricing ?
    Another benefit of choosing Kurian Renovations for your renovation project is that they provide a detailed, fixed-price contract before starting the work. This means that you will know exactly what you are paying for, and that there will be no hidden fees or surprises along the way. Kurian Renovations will outline the scope, timeline, and cost of the project in a clear and transparent manner, and will stick to the agreed terms. This way, you can avoid any misunderstandings or disputes, and enjoy a smooth and hassle-free renovation experience.
  • Is there any Insurance?
    Kurian Renovations is not only a reliable and reputable renovation company, but also a responsible and safe one. They have $2 million in liability insurance, which means that they are covered in case of any accidents or damages that may occur during the renovation process. This gives you peace of mind that you are working with a professional and trustworthy company, and that you are protected from any potential risks or losses. Kurian Renovations takes safety seriously, and ensures that their work meets the highest standards of quality and compliance.
  • How does communication take place?
    Kurian Renovations believes in maintaining a direct line of communication with their clients throughout the renovation project. This means that you will always be able to reach them by phone, email, or text, and that they will keep you updated on the progress and status of the work. You will also be able to share your feedback, questions, or concerns with them at any time, and they will respond promptly and respectfully. Kurian Renovations values your input and involvement, and strives to make you feel comfortable and informed every step of the way.
  • Are payments flexible?
    Kurian Renovations understands that renovation projects can be costly and stressful, and that’s why they offer a progressive payment schedule to their clients. This means that you will only pay for the work that has been completed and approved by you, and that you will have the option to adjust or cancel the project at any time. Kurian Renovations will provide you with regular invoices and receipts, and will accept various methods of payment. Kurian Renovations aims to make your renovation project affordable and flexible, and to meet your expectations and needs.
  • What about Aesthetics?
    Kurian Renovations knows that every renovation project is unique and personal, and that’s why they provide a dedicated design consultant to their clients. This means that you will have a professional and experienced designer who will work with you to create a custom and beautiful design for your space. The design consultant will listen to your ideas and preferences, and will offer you suggestions and solutions that suit your style and budget. The design consultant will also coordinate with the renovation team to ensure that your design is executed flawlessly. Kurian Renovations wants to make your renovation project a dream come true.
