Basement Bathrooms & Kitchenettes

A bathroom or kitchenette is a popular addition to many basements. It can make your space more comfortable and convenient for guests or family members. It can also increase the value of your home when you sell it. Even if you don’t have the plumbing and drainage ready, we can help you install them and make your basement renovation dreams come true!

  • Hobby Room
  • Entertainment Unit

  • Games Room

You can have your own space for your favourite hobby. Whether music, sewing, or cards is your passion, you can have a room dedicated to it.

A gathering space with a television is common in most finish basements. Our team can make this space ideal with a custom cabinet for your flat screen or a projector for movie nights.

By avoiding gym membership fees, a home gym can save you money for years. We can help you build a workout nook or a full gym.

A games room or area can be found in most finished basements. Games rooms can feature pool tables, foosball tables, ping pong, air hockey, and more.

No more dark basements. With the varied selection of pot lights and lighting features, you can have a well-lit basement that will equal the main floor.
