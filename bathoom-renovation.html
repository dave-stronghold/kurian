Bathroom Renovation

Bathrooms that look so good, you would swear they were stock photos. But they’re not! All of the photos on our website are actual renovations that our team has completed over the years. Our most commonly requested renovation is bathrooms, of course, because bathrooms are the most complex room in our homes. In order to pull together a great result, we need our team to work together on demo, plumbing, electrical, HVAC, tiling, painting and carpentry. It’s a tall order for a small space.

Looking for a makeover for your old bathrooms?

From basement bathrooms to loft bathrooms, from home spas to any design you want, the Kurian Renovations team can do it all for you!

Personal Home Luxury

Your bathroom can be a place where you can indulge yourself with a good design and planning. A design that includes a soaker tub, a steam room, or a sauna can make you feel like you are at the spa every day. That’s good for your health and your happiness!

Kurian Renovations, we can help you find designers who can create a bathroom that meets your needs and wants, and then we will build it for you exactly as you want it.

  • Radiant In-Floor Heat
  • Waterproof Potlights
  • Bidets
  • Showers
  • Vanities and Tubs

Many of our clients request radiant in-floor heat as an upgrade. Our team is trained in Schluter/Ditra products and can create a cozy, warm floor that will keep you comfortable in the winter.

Potlights are a great choice for bathrooms, especially waterproof ones over a shower or steam room. A dimmer switch lets you adjust the light level for any occasion.

Bidets that are integrated with your toilet with warm water and heated seats are not just for the rich anymore! They are becoming more popular in our renovations. Please let us know if you want these features at the start of our discussion as they require some planning.

A walk-in shower with a sliding glass door or custom glass shower walls is a great way to replace your small, low bathtubs as your kids grow up. Many of our clients choose this option. We can install shower kits, or a mosaic floor and tiled wall, depending on what you like.

Vanities are becoming more unique and personalized with custom furniture and one or two sinks. With beautiful faucets, the vanity and the freestanding tub stand out as focal points in modern bathrooms.
