Residential Painting

Painting is a great way to refresh your home without spending too much! Our expert team of painters can transform the interior or exterior of your home with a new coat of paint. We also work with commercial clients, such as Pure Kitchen and Bridgehead, who hired us to paint their restaurants and cafes. We have painted complex exteriors and detailed work. We will also paint ceilings, doors and trim. Whatever you need, we’ll paint it! For a bigger paint job, we can visit you on site to review and help you choose colours to achieve the look and feel you want.

Curb appeal is everything. Don’t let your exterior paint ruin your image. Whether it’s peeling, cracked, or painted a horrible colour, you need an upgrade.
